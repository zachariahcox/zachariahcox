## Looking back

### 1\. Accomplishments

* Summarize your top 3 to 5 accomplishments for the period.
* Focus on impact, not activities.  
* Consider using the three circles of impact.
* URLs are good, but don't link to all your PRs.
* For managers, include management and team accomplishments


### 2\. What could have gone better? 

* Apply a growth mindset
* Use the [CARL](https://reflection.ed.ac.uk/reflectors-toolkit/reflecting-on-experience/carl) framework to describe two or three situations where you could have had more impact.   
* What happened? What would you do differently next time? Be specific.

# Peer feedback questions

Nominate 3-to-5 other employees to be asked for feedback.
Give those nominees at least a few days to respond.

* What is your name, role and relation to this person?
* Describe a situation where this person did something really well  
* Describe a situation where this person could have done something better  
* Anything else?
